Amaranthe OFFICIALLY CONFIRM 2025 attendance!

As of the 27th,

Amaranthe have officially confirmed their attendance for the 2025 Lyre Music Festival! This is HUGE! We certainly did'nt expect to have such a big band confirm this early on. Theres still over a year and a half until the next one afterall. However with their confirmation, they have become the lastest big name to sign up. The next fesitval is already shaping up to be one of the best we have ever put together. Amaranthe have always been one of our favourite bands and we were so sorry they couldn't attend the previous festival. Unfortunately the tour dates for their ENORMOUS World Tour; to celebrate the release of their latest smash album "Manifest", clashed with our Festival. Maybe that's why they have confirmed so far in advance this time! In an interview with our very own, Max Arthur, Amaranthe lead singer expressed that she was sorry that the band couldn't make it to the last festival. As of the 10thth,
