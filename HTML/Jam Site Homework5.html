🍇 Product definitions:🍋
Jam:

Jam consists of fruit that is crushed or chopped, cooked with sugar until the pieces of fruit are soft and lose shape. The mixture then thickens as it cooks to a lovely spreadable consistency.

Preserve:

Preserves contain whole fruit or small pieces of fruit in a thick sugar syrup. Perfect for making fruit pies and desserts!

Marmalade:

Marmalade is similiar to Jam but for more bitter citrus fruits. It is made using the juice and peel of the fruit as opposed to the the whole fruit, for a thick, perfectly spreadable, chunky finish. Goes great on Toast!

Fruit Curds:

Fruit Curds are made by cooking down the fruit juice with sugar, rather than using the whole fruit, or any of the peel. This results in a creamier and smoother spread.
